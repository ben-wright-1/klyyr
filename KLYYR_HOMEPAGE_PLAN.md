# KLYYR Homepage Plan

## Goal

Create a landing page that feels minimal, premium, and ingredient-forward while clearly introducing KLYYR's first product: a fluoride-free hydroxyapatite toothpaste.

The page should feel:

- Clear
- Modern
- Quietly confident
- Design-led
- High-trust

## Visual Direction

- Primary palette: black, white, soft gray
- Accent: holographic foil used sparingly for highlights, borders, glow, and motion
- Layout: strong whitespace, large type, clean grid
- Tone: concise, refined, never loud or gimmicky

## Homepage Structure

### 1. Hero

Purpose: introduce the brand and product immediately.

Headline:

`A clearer standard.`

Subhead:

`Modern oral care built around clarity. Fluoride-free hydroxyapatite toothpaste with a cleaner point of view.`

Primary CTA:

`Shop Toothpaste`

Secondary CTA:

`Learn More`

Visual notes:

- Full-bleed black-and-white hero
- Product packshot or tube on a clean reflective surface
- Subtle holographic foil highlight behind or around the product
- Minimal copy, strong whitespace

### 2. Brand Proof Strip

Purpose: give immediate reasons to trust the product.

Suggested items:

- `Fluoride-Free`
- `Hydroxyapatite`
- `Ingredient-Forward`
- `Designed for the Daily Ritual`

Visual notes:

- Thin horizontal strip
- Small uppercase type
- Clean divider lines

### 3. Product Spotlight

Purpose: position the toothpaste as the hero product.

Headline:

`Oral care, refined.`

Body copy:

`KLYYR toothpaste is made for people who want more from the products they use every day. Thoughtfully formulated, visually refined, and designed to bring clarity to your routine.`

Support bullets:

- `Fluoride-free formula`
- `Hydroxyapatite-forward approach`
- `Minimal, premium design`
- `Made for everyday use`

CTA:

`Shop Now`

Visual notes:

- Product image on one side
- Copy and CTA on the other
- Keep this section calm and spacious

### 4. Ingredient Philosophy

Purpose: explain the product philosophy without sounding overly technical.

Headline:

`Every ingredient should have a reason to be here.`

Body copy:

`That is the standard. KLYYR is built around ingredient clarity, thoughtful formulation, and a cleaner approach to everyday essentials. We keep the language simple, the design refined, and the formula intentional.`

Optional micro-copy cards:

- `Clear ingredients`
- `Thoughtfully formulated`
- `Less noise`
- `Better basics`

Visual notes:

- Editorial layout
- Black-and-white base with foil accent line or shimmer detail

### 5. Why KLYYR

Purpose: introduce the brand story and point of view.

Headline:

`Clear by design.`

Body copy:

`KLYYR was created for people who want their everyday products to feel more intentional. We believe oral care can be cleaner, more modern, and more thoughtfully made, from the formula to the packaging on the counter.`

Secondary line:

`Less noise. Better basics.`

Visual notes:

- Brand manifesto feel
- Strong typography and restraint

### 6. Education / Science Section

Purpose: give users a little more confidence around hydroxyapatite.

Headline:

`Ingredient-forward oral care.`

Body copy:

`We believe daily essentials should be easier to trust and easier to understand. KLYYR uses a modern, ingredient-conscious approach designed to support a cleaner oral care routine.`

Optional supporting points:

- `Designed with intention`
- `Clear, considered formulation`
- `Made for modern routines`

Note:

- Keep claims conservative until compliance language is finalized.

### 7. Closing CTA

Purpose: end the page with a clean purchase prompt.

Headline:

`Let's be KLYYR.`

Body copy:

`Minimal formula. Maximum intention.`

CTA:

`Shop Toothpaste`

Visual notes:

- Dark, stripped-back section
- Strong product CTA
- Holographic accent used as a final premium touch

## Voice Guardrails

Use:

- Short sentences
- High signal language
- Calm confidence
- Ingredient-forward framing
- Design-conscious phrasing

Avoid:

- Loud wellness language
- Overexplaining
- Childish toothpaste category tropes
- Claims that need regulatory review
- Trendy or gimmicky phrasing

## Suggested Build Order

1. Replace the current homepage hero copy and CTA with KLYYR messaging.
2. Add a proof strip below the hero.
3. Refine the featured product section for the toothpaste.
4. Add a brand story / ingredient philosophy section.
5. Add a closing CTA section.
6. Apply the black, white, and holographic visual system across the page.

## Notes For Build

- Keep homepage copy concise and modular.
- Make sections editable in the Shopify theme editor where possible.
- Use foil effects with restraint so they feel premium rather than flashy.
- Prioritize mobile readability and clean spacing.
